Summary

///

Following the 20MY (2020 Model Year) launch of the new Pivi/Pivi Pro infotainment system in JLR (Jaguar Land Rover) vehicles, came the 24MY mid-cycle refresh. The proposed update for 24MY included reconfigured vehicle interiors with several physical controls and switchpacks removed. Our team was tasked with designing a solution around relocating those affected features, previously physical controls, into the vehicle's touchscreen. After several design iterations, interactive prototypes, and user tests, our team delivered the design solution that made it into the 24MY production vehicles. This UX (user experience) update to the infotainment system eventually cascaded down into all JLR (Jaguar Land Rover) vehicles for 24MY and beyond.
