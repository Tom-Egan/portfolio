Case Study Summary (TL;DR)

  • Context: Independent designer working within the local dirt track racing community
  • Problem: The racetrack(s) and their drivers lacked a consistent, professional visual presence across weekly promotions, individual branding, and season-level storytelling
  • Role: Designer & Photographer
  • Team: None, solo project(s)
  • Timeframe: 2024-Present
  • Key Deliverables: Weekly race promotion posters, driver hero cards, season championship posters, motion graphics for individual photography promotion
  • Outcomes: Established a more professional and engaging visual presence for events and drivers, delivered memorable keepsakes for drivers, sponsors, and fans
