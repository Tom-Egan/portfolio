  • Company: Jaguar Land Rover
  • Problem: Physical vehicle controls removed, requiring safety-critical functions to move into the touchscreen UI.
  • Constraints: Legal one-touch-activataion requirements, limited sidebar space, modifying existing software architecture.
  • Role: UX Designer â€” UX concepting, interaction design, prototyping, usability testing.
  • Team: 2 UX Designers + UX Research (US & UK).
  • Platform: Pivi/Pivi Pro (embedded vehicle software)
  • Key Solutions: Contextual sidebar buttons, overlays, multi-state controls, quick-adjust drag interactions, pre-trip home tile.
  • Outcome: Shipped on 24MY Range Rover Velar; design and interaction patterns scaled across all Jaguar Land Rover vehicles.
